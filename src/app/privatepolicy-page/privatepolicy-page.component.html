Privacy Policy

At Harmonious Harps, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our students and visitors. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information provided to us through our website and online services. By accessing or using our website and services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, contact details, and other relevant information when you register for our online courses or interact with our website. We may also collect non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and usage data, for analytical purposes and to improve our services.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process your course registration and provide access to our online courses and resources.
  • To communicate with you regarding course updates, important announcements, and relevant information.
  • To personalize your learning experience and provide tailored recommendations based on your preferences and progress.
  • To improve our website, services, and instructional materials through analysis and statistical insights.
  • To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and address any issues or concerns you may have.
  • To send promotional offers, newsletters, and updates related to Harmonious Harps. You may opt-out of such communications at any time.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security and integrity of your personal information. We implement appropriate physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or alteration of the information under our control. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage method is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers, business partners, and contractors who assist us in delivering our services, as necessary to fulfill our obligations to you. We ensure that these third parties maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze usage patterns. You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect certain features and functionalities of our website.

6. Children's Privacy

Harmonious Harps' online courses and services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our records.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website and online materials may contain links to third-party websites or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of such websites before providing any personal information.

8. Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will post the updated version on our website, and the revised policy will become effective upon posting. We encourage you to review the privacy policy periodically to stay informed about our data practices.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us at services@Harmoniousharps.com

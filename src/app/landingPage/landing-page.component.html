Our History

Harmonious Harps is a renowned online harp school dedicated to providing high-quality harp education to aspiring musicians worldwide. Founded in 2010 by Elizabeth Thompson, an accomplished harpist and passionate music educator, the school has garnered a reputation for excellence in harp instruction. With a focus on providing comprehensive online courses, Harmonious Harps has successfully made harp learning accessible to individuals of all ages and skill levels.

Our Fearless Leader

Elizabeth Thompson, a prodigious harpist with a lifelong love for music, discovered her affinity for the harp at a young age. Born into a family deeply rooted in musical traditions, she was exposed to the enchanting sounds of the harp from an early age. Fascinated by its ethereal tones and mesmerizing melodies, Elizabeth began her journey as a harpist under the guidance of her grandmother, who was an accomplished harpist herself.

With a vision to make harp education accessible to anyone with an internet connection, Elizabeth combined her expertise in harp performance and her pedagogical skills to establish Harmonious Harps, an online platform that would revolutionize harp learning. She assembled a team of talented harpists and educators who shared her passion and dedication, ensuring that Harmonious Harps offered the highest standard of instruction.
